Our Story

Built from passion,
driven by results.

POWER ZONE was founded in 2012 by James Carter after a decade working with professional athletes and Fortune 500 executives.

He noticed one pattern: the most successful people don't just work harder — they have a structured system and someone holding them accountable.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of certified coaches serving over 1,200 clients across 40 countries.
